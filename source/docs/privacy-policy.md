# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 2024

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit the Antonella Framework documentation website and use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our documentation website, we may automatically collect:

- **Technical Information:**
  - IP address
  - Browser type and version
  - Operating system
  - Device information
  - Referring website
  - Pages visited and time spent
  - Date and time of visits

- **Usage Analytics:**
  - Page views and navigation patterns
  - Search queries within the documentation
  - Download statistics
  - Error reports and performance metrics

### Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide:

- **GitHub Integration:**
  - GitHub username (when contributing)
  - Public profile information
  - Contribution history

- **Community Participation:**
  - Comments on GitHub issues
  - Discussion forum posts
  - Bug reports and feature requests

- **Contact Information:**
  - Email address (when contacting support)
  - Name (optional, when provided)

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses

We use collected information to:

- **Improve Documentation:**
  - Analyze usage patterns
  - Identify popular content
  - Optimize user experience
  - Fix broken links and errors

- **Provide Support:**
  - Respond to inquiries
  - Troubleshoot issues
  - Provide technical assistance

- **Enhance Security:**
  - Detect and prevent abuse
  - Monitor for security threats
  - Protect against spam and malicious activity

### Analytics and Improvements

- Track documentation effectiveness
- Identify areas for improvement
- Understand user needs and preferences
- Optimize site performance

## Information Sharing

### We Do NOT Share

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing

We may share information only in these circumstances:

- **Service Providers:**
  - Analytics services (Google Analytics)
  - Hosting providers
  - CDN services
  - Only for providing our services

- **Legal Requirements:**
  - When required by law
  - To protect our rights
  - To ensure user safety
  - In response to valid legal requests

- **Business Transfers:**
  - In case of merger or acquisition
  - With appropriate privacy protections

## Data Security

### Protection Measures

We implement appropriate security measures:

- **Technical Safeguards:**
  - Encryption in transit (HTTPS)
  - Secure hosting infrastructure
  - Regular security updates
  - Access controls and monitoring

- **Administrative Safeguards:**
  - Limited access to personal information
  - Staff training on privacy practices
  - Regular security reviews

## Cookies and Tracking

### Types of Cookies

We use different types of cookies:

- **Essential Cookies:**
  - Required for basic site functionality
  - Cannot be disabled
  - Session management and security

- **Analytics Cookies:**
  - Track usage and performance
  - Help improve user experience
  - Can be disabled

- **Preference Cookies:**
  - Remember your settings
  - Enhance user experience
  - Optional

### Cookie Management

You can manage cookies through:

- Browser settings
- Cookie consent tools
- Third-party opt-out tools

See our [Cookie Policy](cookie-policy) for detailed information.
